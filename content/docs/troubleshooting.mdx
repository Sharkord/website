  <AccordionItem title="Voice and video not working">
    Make sure you:

    - are in a secure context (HTTPS or http://localhost)
    - have allowed microphone and camera permissions in your browser
    - have configured the correct `rtcMinPort` and `rtcMaxPort` in your `config.ini` and opened those ports in your firewall/router for UDP traffic.

  </AccordionItem>

    <AccordionItem title="I keep getting disconnected from the server (code 1006)">
    This is most likely a network issue. Please read the following issues for possible solutions:
    - [https://github.com/Sharkord/sharkord/issues/46](https://github.com/Sharkord/sharkord/issues/46)
    - [https://github.com/Sharkord/sharkord/issues/289](https://github.com/Sharkord/sharkord/issues/289)

  </AccordionItem>
