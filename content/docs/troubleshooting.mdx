<AccordionItem title="Voice and video not working">
Make sure you:
- are in a secure context (HTTPS or http://localhost)
- have allowed microphone and camera permissions in your browser
- have configured the correct `webRtc.port` in your `config.ini` and opened those ports in your firewall/router for UDP traffic. In some setups, you might also need to set `webRtc.announcedAddress` to your public IP address or domain name, especially if you're behind a NAT.

</AccordionItem>
