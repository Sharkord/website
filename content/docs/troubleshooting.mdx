  <AccordionItem title="Voice and video not working">
    Make sure you:

    - are in a secure context (HTTPS or http://localhost)
    - have allowed microphone and camera permissions in your browser
    - have configured the correct `rtcMinPort` and `rtcMaxPort` in your `config.ini` and opened those ports in your firewall/router for UDP traffic.

  </AccordionItem>
